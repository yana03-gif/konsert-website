EVENT DESCRIPTION

Join us for a night of heartfelt melodies and soulful performance at the “Konsert Galau” on 21 December 2024 at Autocity Juru from 8 p.m until 12 a.m. This concert promises an immersive musical experience that delves into the depth of life’s emotional terrain, with a line up of talented artists and famous musician for their abilty to express the essence of longing and introspection. Embrace vulnerability and connect through shared experience at this event, which offers a space to find relief in emotional lyrics or soulful songs. Come experience a night of moving songs and passionate performance as we explore the intricacies of melancholy together.
